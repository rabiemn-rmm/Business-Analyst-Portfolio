## Problem Statement 
There is an absence in live tracking of donation campaigns in Lebanon, leading to unorganized workflows, poor donor experiences, and mistakes in stock management. levels 
## Goals
To create a system that displays:
- Real-time display of donation milestones, such as medical interview, physical examination, donation duration, and post donation information.
- Real-time stock level display with checkpoints for delivery and reception of units
